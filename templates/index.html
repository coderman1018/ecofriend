The Big Problem

We all share one big planet Earth and sometimes...that can be a big problem. People have different needs and desires, but again, we do all share the same place. Trash in the ocean, cutting down trees, throwing garbage anywhere and everywhere...these are things people do in their daily lives without giving it a second thought. It is our duty, as residents and citizens of Planet Earth, to protect our environment from getting dirtier and dirtier every day. We need to make sure the beauty doesn't turn ugly. Of course, there are some aspects that we can't control, like natural disasters (earthquaks, tsunamis...), but we can do our personal best to try and keep the world amazing for generations and generations to come.
